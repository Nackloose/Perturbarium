It exemplifies how Inkspill serves as a safe space for ambitious, half-formed ideasâ€”where technical imagination is encouraged to run ahead of implementation, and where even radical or unconventional proposals are welcome. > *"Some notes are just future footnotes to a story you haven't written yet. Some are the first lines of a new chapter in the field."*
